Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Vajra Labs ("we," "our," "us") provides an automated client outreach tool for businesses and professionals in India. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how it's protected.

Information we collect

  • Account details: your name, email address, and business information you provide during setup
  • Gmail credentials (app password) used only to send outreach emails and check replies on your behalf, stored encrypted
  • WhatsApp Business connection details, if you connect a WhatsApp number
  • Lead data: business names, emails, phone numbers, and addresses discovered through Google Maps and other sources you configure
  • Payment information processed through Razorpay; we do not store your card or UPI details ourselves

How we use this information

  • To operate the outreach features you request: finding leads, sending emails and WhatsApp messages, checking replies
  • To process your subscription payment and keep your account active
  • To improve the product and respond to support requests

Third-party services we use

We rely on trusted third parties to run the service, including Google Maps (lead discovery), Supabase (account and data storage), Razorpay (payments), and Meta/WhatsApp (messaging, where connected). Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing the data they process.

Data retention

We retain your account and lead data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your personal information, or ask us questions about this policy, by contacting us at the details on our Contact Us page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
