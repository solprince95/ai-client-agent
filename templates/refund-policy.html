Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Free trial

New accounts get a 5-day free trial with full access. No payment is required to start the trial, so there's nothing to refund during this period.

Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time from the Billing tab in your dashboard. Cancelling stops all future automatic charges. You'll continue to have access for the remainder of the billing cycle you've already paid for.

Refunds

Since Vajra Labs is a monthly subscription providing immediate access to the full service, we generally don't offer refunds for the current billing cycle once a payment has been successfully charged. If you believe you were charged in error, such as a duplicate charge or a charge after you'd already cancelled, contact us and we'll review it promptly.

How to request a refund review

Reach out through our Contact Us page with your registered email and the payment date. We aim to respond within 2 to 3 business days.

Failed or disputed payments

If an automatic renewal charge fails, Razorpay may retry it a few times before your subscription lapses. If you dispute a charge with your bank without contacting us first, this can delay any resolution, so we encourage reaching out to us directly first.
